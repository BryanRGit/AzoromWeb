Training

We provide Technical Training and General Management and Development Training for Generation, Transmission and Distribution.

Our approach is focussed and consultative and this permeates each step of our offering:-

  • Training Needs Assessment - We focus on ensuring that the Training is effective and meets the real needs of the organisation
  • Training Design is targeted towards organisation and employee needs to maximise impact and value
  • Training Delivery is by professionals with many years experience in the field and specifically in depth understanding of Electricity industry and it's needs
