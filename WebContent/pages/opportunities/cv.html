Curriculum Vitae Format

Please submit your Curriculum Vitae to the following format:

CV Item Guideline
Name First Name first followed by SURNAME (in upper case)
Address Contact address
Phone Numbers Indicate if Landline or Mobile. Always give your Country Prefix
Date of Birth
Nationality If you have joint citizenship list both
Languages List all indicating proficiency in Speaking, Reading, Writing
e.g. English: Speaking - fluent, Reading - good, Writing - basic
Countries Worked In Simply detail countries you have previously worked in
Core Competencies List here in brief what you regard as your signature skills, strengths
Experience Start with the latest first
Show period employed (e.g. Jan 2004 to March 2005)
Employer Organisation Name and Location
Employment Location
Title of position held
Area of responsibility and tasks
Details of responsibility and tasks
Achievements, Experiences (Technical and Organisational)
Education Detail all formal education - Latest first
Indicate Years, Institution and formal qualifications obtained
Professional Development Include here any significant training or additional formal skills acquired
IT Proficiencies Detail extent of IT knowledge or experiences
